Confidentiality Agreement

Our language translation professionals and project management team will take care of your translation assignment with absolute confidentiality. We are proud of our clearly defined confidentiality processes, and our secure electronic transfer of documents, materials and other information.

Midwest Translation Services and its subsidiaries use private internal technical systems that are updated constantly to ensure absolute protection at all times. All information provided by our clients for translation is always considered confidential and treated as such, regardless of the nature of the exchange. All client information remains the intellectual property of the client and will be held strictly confidential by our entire team of professionals.

Swift & Accurate Services

Every document is analyzed, deciphered, translated, formatted, and then proofread several times. By the time a document reaches the certification/approval stage with our lead translators, the new translated version is a perfect match of the original.

Our company leverages internet technologies along with the talented minds of our translation staff to deliver high quality translation projects in the shortest amount of time. In addition to our in-house experts, many of our certified translators are located across global time zones to ensure we have a 24/7 “around-the-clock” translation team.

Our Values

Strive for excellence. Encourage understanding of various cultures. We want to become a conduit that will help bridge the gap between different cultures. Practice thinking outside the box. We are constantly looking for ways to improve our work and our business services for our clients.

Quality

Our Quality Assurance Program consists of the following steps to ensure we achieve the highest standards of quality for our clients:

  • Creation of a glossary for your in-house linguists’ or distributors’ review
  • Translation by native-speaking, professionally-trained translators of the target languages, each of whom also specializes in your field or industry. Manage translators to facilitate that multilingual projects all work simultaneously
  • Consultation with bilingual industry specialists, if needed, to clarify terminology
  • Translation/Document Edit: Proofreading for spelling, punctuation and grammar
  • Formatting to correspond to the design of the source files as closely as possible
  • Multilingual desktop publishing (DTP) (if required)
  • Final, post-layout proofing
