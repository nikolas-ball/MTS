Remote Interpreting Services

Connect with qualified interpreters instantly via phone or video

Why Choose Remote Interpreting?

Remote interpreting allows people to connect more quickly than ever before. In today's fast-paced world, it's a relief to begin communicating effectively within seconds. Phone or video interpreting may be the right choice for you and your clients:

  • When an interpreter is needed immediately
  • For making reminder calls that help reduce no-show appointments
  • In rural areas where qualified local interpreters are limited
  • When social distancing is required
  • When you need less common languages, including Indigenous languages from Mexico and Guatemala (with advance scheduling)
  • To help reduce interpreter travel time, costs, and minimum fees

How It Works

Our remote interpreting service is simple and efficient. Choose between:

  • Phone Interpreting: Quick connections for immediate communication needs
  • Video Interpreting: Face-to-face interaction with video capability for more personal connections

Interpreter Qualifications

*A qualified interpreter is someone who has earned an entry-level certificate through an accredited organization and has demonstrated proficiency in English and all working languages through diplomas and/or testing.

We work ONLY with qualified/certified interpreters. We reserve the right to cancel video meetings should interpreter services not match with our needs.

Who We Are

Our Mission

Midwest Translation Services, LLC's mission is to provide superior language services for companies and individual clients.

Our Values

Strive for excellence. Encourage understanding of various cultures. We want to become a conduit that will help bridge the gap between different cultures. Practice thinking outside the box. We are constantly looking for ways to improve our work and our business services for our clients.

Quality

Our Quality Assurance Program consists of the following steps to ensure we achieve the highest standards of quality for our clients:

  • Creation of a glossary for your in-house linguists' or distributors' review
  • Translation by native-speaking, professionally-trained translators of the target languages, each of whom also specializes in your field or industry. Manage translators to facilitate that multilingual projects all work simultaneously
  • Consultation with bilingual industry specialists, if needed, to clarify terminology
  • Translation/Document Edit: Proofreading for spelling, punctuation and grammar
  • Formatting to correspond to the design of the source files as closely as possible
  • Multilingual desktop publishing (DTP) (if required)
  • Final, post-layout proofing

Confidentiality

We do everything we can to protect the confidentiality of our client's information. Our linguists are bound by confidentiality agreements to ensure the privacy of our clients.
